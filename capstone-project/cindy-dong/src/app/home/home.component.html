About:

This project is centered around the analysis and visualization of data related to uninsured individuals under the age of 65 in various counties within Virginia. The primary objective is to understand the distribution and factors influencing the lack of health insurance coverage in these areas.

Dataset:

The data for this project is sourced from the Agency for Healthcare Research and Qualityâ€™s (AHRQ) Social Determinants of Health (SDOH) Database (Beta Version). This database provides a comprehensive set of variables across various domains, facilitating research and analysis on social determinants of health.

Trends:

  • Year: This variable helps track changes and trends over time.
  • Total Weighted Population: This variable provides a measure of the total population in each county, accounting for factors such as age distribution.
  • Geography: This variable indicates whether a county is coastal or not, which can influence access to healthcare services.
